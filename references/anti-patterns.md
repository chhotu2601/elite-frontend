# Anti-Patterns by Industry

Things that scream "AI made this." Avoid at all costs.

## Universal Anti-Patterns (Never Do These)

- ❌ Purple/blue gradient backgrounds (THE #1 AI slop tell)
- ❌ Emojis as section icons (use SVG: Heroicons, Lucide, Phosphor)
- ❌ "Powered by AI" aesthetic when the product isn't about AI
- ❌ Generic stock photo heroes
- ❌ Centered-everything layouts with no visual hierarchy
- ❌ More than 3 fonts
- ❌ Rainbow color schemes with no intentional palette
- ❌ Walls of text with no visual breaks
- ❌ Hero → 3 cards → pricing → footer (the AI template layout)
- ❌ "Get Started" as the only CTA copy (be specific: "Start Free Trial", "See Pricing", "Book a Demo")

## By Industry

### SaaS / Tech
- ❌ Purple gradients
- ❌ Generic dashboard mockups
- ❌ "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing" copy
- ✅ Dark mode works well
- ✅ Show the actual product, not illustrations of it
- ✅ Social proof (logos, metrics) above the fold

### Finance / Banking
- ❌ Neon colors, playful fonts
- ❌ Crypto-bro aesthetics
- ❌ AI purple/pink gradients
- ✅ Navy, forest green, gold accents
- ✅ Serif fonts for headings (trust signals)
- ✅ Clean data visualization

### Healthcare / Wellness
- ❌ Dark mode
- ❌ Harsh, saturated colors
- ❌ Technical jargon-heavy design
- ✅ Soft palettes (sage, cream, soft blue)
- ✅ Generous whitespace
- ✅ Calming typography (light weights)

### E-commerce / Retail
- ❌ Cluttered hero with too many products
- ❌ Stock photos of models
- ✅ Product photography > everything
- ✅ Social proof above fold (reviews, trust badges)
- ✅ Clear pricing, no hidden info

### Creative / Portfolio
- ❌ Generic grid of thumbnails
- ❌ "Welcome to my portfolio"
- ✅ Let the work speak first
- ✅ Dramatic imagery, bold typography
- ✅ Unique layouts (asymmetric, editorial)

### Developer Tools
- ❌ Overly corporate feel
- ✅ Dark mode with syntax-highlighted code blocks
- ✅ Monospace font accents
- ✅ Terminal/CLI aesthetic elements
- ✅ Clear documentation links
